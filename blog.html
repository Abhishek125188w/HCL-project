Indian painting has been around for a pretty long time as we can find the pictures in many scriptures and books which speaks about the art. Tanjore painting is one of the famous Indian paintings which is adorned with gold foils and precious jems and stones. Likewise many Indian artists show the beauty of India through their Indian paintings.
